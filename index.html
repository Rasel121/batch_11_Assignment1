How do i schedule an Appointments

Scheduling an appointment at Medi Bridge is easy. You can call our office directly or use our online appointment booking system on our website. Simply select your preferred date and time, and our staff will confirm your appointment promptly.

What types of healthcare services does we offer?

Medi Bridge offers a variety of services including general checkups, pediatrics, women’s health, and chronic disease care. We also provide lab testing, immunizations, and telehealth consultations. Our goal is to deliver complete and compassionate care for all ages.

Are walk-in appointments available?

Yes, walk-in appointments are available based on our daily schedule. However, we recommend booking in advance to reduce waiting time. Emergency cases are always prioritized regardless of appointments.

Does Medi Bridge accept insurance plans?
